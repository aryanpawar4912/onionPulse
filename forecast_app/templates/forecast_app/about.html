Our Mission

Reducing Volatility in India's Onion Market

Onion prices in India can swing by over 300% in a single season — devastating farmers while shocking consumers. OnionPulse AI was created to bring predictability, transparency, and data intelligence to this critical market.

Our system analyzes decades of historical APMC data, weather patterns, seasonal trends, and supply chain signals to generate accurate forward-looking price forecasts for farmers, traders, and policymakers alike.

Machine learning ensemble of LSTM, XGBoost, Random Forest & Prophet models
Real-time APMC market data from 8+ major Maharashtra mandis
Weather & monsoon pattern integration for superior seasonal accuracy
Personalized recommendations for farmers, traders & policymakers
Challenge & Solution

The Problem We Solve

India's onion market is plagued by extreme price volatility — causing ripple effects across the entire agri-economy.

Market Problems

  • Farmers suffer huge losses during sudden price crashes
  • Consumers face shock price spikes — up to 300% swings
  • Traders struggle with inventory planning & procurement
  • Government policies often react too late to prevent damage
  • No reliable data tool existed for price prediction
  • Seasonal and weather impacts are unpredictable

Our AI Solution

  • Ensemble of 4 ML models for maximum prediction accuracy
  • Real-time data ingestion from 8+ APMC markets daily
  • Weather pattern & monsoon integration in forecasting
  • Personalized buy/sell/hold recommendations by user type
  • 85%+ accuracy rate validated on historical test data
  • Government-ready policy intelligence dashboards
