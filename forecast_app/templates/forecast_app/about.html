Our Mission

We aim to reduce instability in India's onion market using AI powered price forecasting.

Onion prices fluctuate drastically causing losses for farmers and high prices for consumers. Our system analyzes historical data, seasonal patterns, weather impact and market trends to forecast onion prices with high accuracy.
