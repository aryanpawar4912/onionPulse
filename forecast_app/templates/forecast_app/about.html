About Onion Price Forecast

Revolutionizing onion market stability through AI and machine learning

Our Mission

To reduce market instability in the Indian onion sector by providing accurate, AI-powered price predictions that empower farmers, traders, consumers, and policymakers.

Onion prices in India are notoriously volatile, causing significant problems across the supply chain. Our platform leverages cutting-edge machine learning algorithms to analyze historical data, market factors, and seasonal patterns to forecast prices with unprecedented accuracy.

The Problem
Market Instability Challenges
  • Farmers suffer losses during price crashes
  • Consumers face price shocks during shortages
  • Traders struggle with inventory management
  • Government reactive measures often come too late
  • Price fluctuations up to 300% within a single season
Our Solution
AI-Powered Forecasting
  • Multiple ML models (LSTM, Random Forest, XGBoost, Prophet)
  • Real-time data integration from APMC markets
  • Weather and seasonal analysis
  • 85%+ prediction accuracy for 7-day forecasts
  • Personalized recommendations for different user types
